                                                                                                    Dr. Snehanshu Pal, Head, Research Lab

Dr. Snehanshu Pal is an assistant professor of Metallurgical and Materials Engineering Department , National Institute of Technology Rourkela. He has served as Post-Doctoral Fellow in the Department of Materials Science and Engineering, The Pennsylvania State University, USA. He has done Ph.D. in Metallurgical and Materials Engineering from Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur, India.

My research group focuses on the study of deformation behaviour of nano structured material using Molecular Dynamics simulation, Material Informatics and Modeling of Metallurgical Processes.
